What is the Universal AI Prompt Generator?

The Universal AI Prompt Generator is a powerful VS Code/Cursor IDE extension that revolutionizes how you interact with AI assistants. This professional-grade tool transforms basic queries into expertly-crafted, structured prompts using proven methodologies from AI research and prompt engineering best practices.
